About me

Hi, I'm Krishna Thakar, a dedicated Computer Science student specializing in Data Science at Southeast Missouri State University. With a strong academic foundation and a 3.9 GPA, I've consistently earned recognition through placements on both the Dean's List and President's List.

My professional journey blends deep expertise in machine learning, data analytics, and software engineering, showcased through diverse projects and research presentations. I thrive on solving complex real-world problems by harnessing powerful technologies like Python, R, PostgreSQL, and advanced machine learning frameworks such as PyTorch and TensorFlow.

Among my significant accomplishments, I led a team to victory at the ASA DataFest 2025, earning the "Best Use of Statistical Analysis" award by analyzing over 200,000 commercial lease records using predictive modeling techniques. My research on sentiment analysis using transformer-based models, specifically RoBERTa, earned notable recognition at the Southeast Missouri State University Student Research Conference.

My passion for AI extends to innovative applications, like developing StealthChess.AI, a real-time chess assistant powered by YOLOv8, OpenCV, and Stockfish, delivering live strategic insights through smart glasses. Additionally, I developed Echo Chamber, a news recommendation system addressing media bias using NLP and TF-IDF methods, significantly enhancing user experience with personalized and balanced news consumption.

Beyond research and AI applications, I've delivered impactful software solutions, including SEMO Esports, a comprehensive full-stack web platform that streamlined resource management, significantly reducing congestion and operational inefficiencies.

My commitment to continuous learning is supported by certifications in supervised machine learning and exploratory data analysis from Deeplearning.AI and IBM, respectively. Through my experience as an IT support specialist and Resident Assistant, I've developed strong skills in problem-solving, communication, and leadership.

I'm deeply driven to leverage technology to make data accessible, insightful, and transformative. Let's connect to explore potential collaborations or opportunities to innovate together.
